The Evolution is FUN! project aims to bring games about evolution and ecology into the classrooms, or to any occasion where people are open for such games. The project is funded by the European Society for Evolutionary Biology.

There were four major parts of the project: 1) we collected our existing games and designed many more 2) we created this website, 3) we held several free workshops between 2020-2022, where we introduced these games to all of those interested and motivated to use them and 4) we have published a booklet with games, tips and links.

The booklet can be downloaded from here: Booklet (A4, pdf)


We hope you will enjoy browsing this page and the games!
